Jim Nielsen, Web Generalist

Designer, Front-end Engineer, Writer

I love the web. I believe the web browser is the most influential piece of software ever written, and I’ve spent 20+ years designing and building experiences around it.

I love being at the intersection or art and engineering — where people don’t ask, “I thought you were a designer, why are you writing code?” Or vice versa.
